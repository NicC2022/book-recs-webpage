Nic's Book Recommendations

Bet On Yourself: From zero to millions

Dr. Vernard L Hodges

Dr. Hodges explains how to create multiple streams of income, what the stock market is and how it works, how to get started investing in rental properties, and the importance of doing the right thing when no one is looking.

Bonds

Brandon Braxton

Detectives Alicia Parsons faces her tumultuous family history, while a mix of old and new allies joins her in a thrilling investigation. She embarks on her most challenging case, ensuring an exhilarating and suspenseful read.

Drift House: The First Voyage

Dale Peck

After 9/11, the Oakenfeld children move to Uncle Farley's peculiar Drift House on The Bay of Eternity, which drifts into the Sea of Time. They face mermaids, pirates, and a secret plot with time at stake in this thrilling adventure.

Dirty Little Secrets About Black History : Its Heroes & Other Troublemakers

Claud Anderson

This eye-opening book challenges the omission of black history in traditional narratives. It reveals how black labor drove nations and shaped history, from industrial revolutions to social upheavals. Uncovering hidden truths, it sheds light on America's complex past.

The Four Agreements: A Practical Guide to Personal Freedom (A Toltec Wisdom Book)

Don Miguel Ruiz

This insightful book draws inspiration from Toltec spiritual beliefs and offers readers a path to explore "freedom," "happiness," and "love." It emphasizes that our lives are shaped by the agreements we make with ourselves, others, and society.

Whisper (Alicia Parsons Cases Book 1)

Brandon Braxton

In Brandon Braxton's debut novella, Detective Alicia Parsons faces her first case involving a string of gruesome murders. As the past collides with the present, she must solve the case to prevent further deaths, but the stakes are higher than she could have imagined.

Rad Women Worldwide: Artists and Athletes, Pirates and Punks, and Other Revolutionaries Who Shaped History

Kate Schatz

Rad Women Worldwide" is an inspiring collection of diverse women's stories, from Hatshepsut to Malala Yousafzai and beyond. With captivating biographies and cut-paper portraits, it celebrates their incredible achievements, making it an essential addition to women's history.

The Richest Man in Babylon

George S. Cleason

Discover the ancient secrets of prosperity in "The Richest Man in Babylon." George S. Clason's timeless book shares the wisdom of the Babylonians on wealth creation, debt management, investments, and lasting financial success.

Universe Glow #1: The Universians (Universe Glow: The Anthology)

In an alternate Earth, supernatural forces are intertwined with science. Humans, Elves, Wiccans, and more coexist, but the arrival of the powerful Universians disrupts the world's equilibrium. Dive into the fascinating and turbulent Universian lifestyle in this chapter-story.
